All About E

If you've made it this far that means you completed the short

and hopefully fun questions about me. My name is Eric although

many people call me E or Bygg E for short. I was born and raised

in Memphis,TN and attended East High where I'm an alumni. Earned

several athletic scholarships to a number of Division 1 colleges.

I chose to attend University of Cincinnati where Psychology was

my major with a minor in Biology. After college I held a series of

jobs ranging from a clothing retailer to a compounding engineer.

Gaining forklift certification to operate any type and brand of

forklift. My goals are fairly simple, well for me they are.

Continue to add to my skill set which covers all areas of my life.

With the knowlege that I've gained and continue to gain I want to

help build my community to better than what it ever was, build my own

business empire, and to leave all that I've earned to my children.

A legacy that not even my wildest imagination could conjure up.
